<h1> RPDR FANTASY LEAGUE </h1>

<h2> Gameplay: </h2>
<p> Throughout the Season, players' queens will compete and earn their players some points. </p>

<h3> Point Breakdown </h3>

<h4> Adding Points </h4>
<ul>
    <li>Winning a Maxi Challenge: 15 points </li>
    <li>Winning the Snatch Game: 20 points </li>
    <li>Winning the Mini Challenge: 10 points</li>
    <li>Placing in the Tops of the week: 10 points</li>
    <li>Being safe this week: 5 points</li>
    <li>Winning the lipsynch: 10 points</li>
    <li>Having a good runway (toot): 10 points</li>
    <li>Having an iconic/memeable moment: 10 points</li>
</ul>

<h4> Losing Points </h4>
<ul>
    <li>Placing in the bottom 3: -5 points</li>
    <li>Placing in the bottom 2: -10 points</li>
    <li>Elimination: -15 points</li>
    <li>Having a bad runway (boot): -5 points</li>
    <li>Having a cringeworthy moment: -5 points</li>
</ul>
